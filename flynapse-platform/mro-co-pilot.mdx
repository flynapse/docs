# MRO Co-Pilot - Unified Search & Chat Interface

The MRO Co-Pilot is Flynapse's flagship AI assistant designed specifically for Maintenance, Repair, and Operations (MRO) engineers. It enables seamless interaction with complex technical documentation through an intuitive conversational interface.

## Overview

The MRO Co-Pilot transforms how aviation maintenance professionals access and interact with technical documentation. Instead of manually searching through thousands of pages of AMMs, SBs, IPCs, and ADs, engineers can simply ask questions in natural language and receive intelligent, contextual responses.

### Key Benefits

- **Time Savings**: Find relevant information in seconds instead of hours
- **Improved Accuracy**: AI-powered search reduces human error
- **Enhanced Collaboration**: Share findings and add comments to documents
- **Contextual Understanding**: AI understands aviation terminology and procedures
- **Document Intelligence**: Automatic summarization and comparison capabilities

## Use Cases

### 1. Routine Maintenance Procedures

**Scenario**: Engineer needs to perform routine maintenance on landing gear

**Workflow:**
1. Ask: "Show me landing gear inspection procedures"
2. Filter by aircraft type: A320
3. Select relevant AMM documents
4. Request summary of procedures
5. Share findings with team

### 2. Emergency Repairs

**Scenario**: Aircraft has thrust reverser issue requiring immediate attention

**Workflow:**
1. Search: "thrust reverser troubleshooting"
2. Filter by document type: AMM, SB
3. Review emergency procedures
4. Add comments for team awareness
5. Download relevant procedures

### 3. Regulatory Compliance

**Scenario**: New airworthiness directive requires immediate action

**Workflow:**
1. Search: "AD 2024-01-15 compliance"
2. Review affected aircraft types
3. Compare with existing procedures
4. Generate compliance checklist
5. Share with maintenance team

**Scenario**: New engineer needs to learn specific procedures

1. Search: "engine removal training"

4. Add training notes as comments
5. Create knowledge base entry

## Best Practices

### 1. Effective Querying

- **Be Specific**: "A320 landing gear inspection" vs "landing gear"
- **Use Aviation Terms**: Leverage standard aviation terminology
- **Include Context**: Mention aircraft type, system, or component
- **Ask Follow-ups**: Refine searches based on initial results

### 2. Document Management

- **Select Relevant Documents**: Choose documents that match your needs
- **Use Filters**: Apply document type and aircraft filters early
- **Review Summaries**: Use AI summaries to quickly assess relevance
- **Add Comments**: Contribute to team knowledge base

### 3. Collaboration

- **Share Findings**: Use email sharing for important discoveries
- **Add Comments**: Document insights and observations
- **Vote on Comments**: Help identify valuable information
- **Maintain Context**: Reference previous conversations when relevant
