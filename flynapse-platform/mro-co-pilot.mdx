# MRO Co-Pilot - Unified Search & Chat Interface

The MRO Co-Pilot is Flynapse's flagship AI assistant designed specifically for Maintenance, Repair, and Operations (MRO) engineers. It enables seamless interaction with complex technical documentation through an intuitive conversational interface.

## Overview

The MRO Co-Pilot transforms how aviation maintenance professionals access and interact with technical documentation. Instead of manually searching through thousands of pages of AMMs, SBs, IPCs, and ADs, engineers can simply ask questions in natural language and receive intelligent, contextual responses.

### Key Benefits

- **Time Savings**: Find relevant information in seconds instead of hours
- **Improved Accuracy**: AI-powered search reduces human error
- **Enhanced Collaboration**: Share findings and add comments to documents
- **Contextual Understanding**: AI understands aviation terminology and procedures
- **Document Intelligence**: Automatic summarization and comparison capabilities

## Best Practices & Optimization

### 1. üéØ Effective Querying Strategies

**Query Optimization:**
- **Be Specific**: "A320 landing gear inspection procedures" vs "landing gear"
- **Use Aviation Terminology**: Leverage standard aviation terms (AMM, SB, AD, IPC)
- **Include Context**: Mention aircraft type, system, component, and maintenance type
- **Progressive Refinement**: Start broad, then narrow down with follow-up questions
- **Use Filters Early**: Apply document type and aircraft filters before asking questions

**Advanced Query Techniques:**
- **Multi-Part Questions**: "Compare A320 vs B737 landing gear inspection procedures"
- **Compliance Queries**: "Show me all ADs affecting thrust reverser systems"
- **Troubleshooting**: "What are common causes of hydraulic system leaks in A320?"
- **Tool Requirements**: "What special tools are needed for engine removal?"

### 2. üìö Document Management Excellence
