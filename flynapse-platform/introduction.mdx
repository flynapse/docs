# Welcome to Flynapse Platform

Flynapse is a cutting-edge AI platform designed specifically for the aviation industry, providing intelligent copilots that revolutionize how airlines, MRO facilities, and aviation professionals interact with complex technical documentation and operational data.

## What is Flynapse?

Flynapse is an intelligent AI platform that combines the power of artificial intelligence with deep domain expertise in aviation. Our platform enables aviation professionals to:

- **Intelligently search** through complex technical documentation (AMM, SB, IPC, AD)
- **Conversationally interact** with maintenance procedures and operational data
- **Collaborate effectively** through document commenting and sharing
- **Scale operations** with AI-powered automation and insights
- **Ensure compliance** with aviation standards and regulations

## Core AI Copilots

### ✈️ MRO Co-Pilot
**Purpose**: Enable airline/MRO engineers to interact with complex technical documentation through conversational AI

**Key Features**:
- **Unified Search & Chat Interface**: Natural language queries across AMM, SB, IPC, AD documents
- **Intelligent Document Filtering**: Filter by document type (AMM, SB, AD, IPC) and aircraft type (A320, B737, etc.)
- **Document Selection & Summarization**: Select relevant documents and get AI-powered summaries
- **Advanced Document Viewer**: Full-featured PDF viewer with hierarchical navigation and commenting
- **Collaborative Features**: Page-level comments, voting, and sharing capabilities

### 🛩️ AOCC Co-Pilot (Coming Soon)
**Purpose**: Assist Airline Operations Control Center with real-time decision making

### 🎧 Cabin Co-Pilot (Coming Soon)
**Purpose**: Support cabin crew with operational procedures and passenger management

## Key Platform Features

### 🔍 Intelligent Search & Retrieval
- **Semantic Search**: Understand context and intent behind queries
- **Multi-Document Search**: Search across entire document libraries
- **Real-time Filtering**: Dynamic filtering by document type, aircraft, and other metadata
- **Relevant Page Detection**: Automatically open documents to the most relevant page

### 💬 Conversational AI Interface
- **Natural Language Processing**: Ask questions in plain English
- **Context-Aware Responses**: AI remembers conversation context and document selections
- **Follow-up Queries**: Refine searches and ask for comparisons or summaries
- **Document Reuse**: Reuse previously found documents in new conversations

### 📄 Advanced Document Management
- **Hierarchical Navigation**: Multi-level table of contents with auto-highlighting
- **Lazy Loading**: High-performance document rendering
- **Search Within Documents**: Ctrl+F functionality with match highlighting
- **Document Metadata**: Comprehensive document information and version tracking

### 👥 Collaboration & Sharing
- **Page-Level Comments**: Add public or private comments on specific pages
- **Comment Feed**: View and interact with comments from team members
- **Voting System**: Like/dislike comments with live upvote counts
- **Document Sharing**: Share documents via email with custom messages
- **Copy to Clipboard**: Quick copying of summaries and responses

### 🛡️ Enterprise Security
- **Role-Based Access Control**: Granular permissions for different user types
- **Audit Logging**: Comprehensive tracking of all user actions
- **Data Encryption**: End-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest
- **Compliance Ready**: Built to meet aviation industry security standards

## Getting Started

### For MRO Engineers
1. **Access the Platform**: Login to your Flynapse account
2. **Select MRO Co-Pilot**: Choose from available copilots
3. **Start Searching**: Ask questions about maintenance procedures
4. **Review Documents**: Select and interact with relevant documents
5. **Collaborate**: Add comments and share findings with your team

### For System Administrators
1. **User Management**: Set up user accounts and permissions
2. **Document Upload**: Upload and index technical documentation
3. **Access Control**: Configure role-based access for different user types
4. **Monitor Usage**: Track platform usage and performance metrics
